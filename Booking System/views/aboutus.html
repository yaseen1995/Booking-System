About Us

We are a dedicated to ensuring you have the most amazing time every time you visit one of our bowling alleys! Bowling is a great time out and everyone can enjoy it. Computerised scoring screens and lanes with ramps and bumpers will give fun and enjoyment to the whole family. As bowling is such a fantastic leisure activity, our site contain a licensed bar, serve food, and have pool tables and many state of the art arcades. You'll always have something to keep you entertained at our centre.
